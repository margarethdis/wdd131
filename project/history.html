Origins of Hangul

Hangul was created by King Sejong in 1443 to improve literacy among common people. It was designed to be simple and easy to learn, unlike Classical Chinese characters that were difficult for the general population to master.

The Evolution of Hangul

Since its creation, Hangul has gone through several stages of reform and modernization. From its initial form to the standardized version used today, Hangul has continually adapted to meet the needs of the Korean language and its speakers.
